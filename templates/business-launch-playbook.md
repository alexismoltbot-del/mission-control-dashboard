# Business Launch Playbook

## Etape 1 - Validation 48h

- Probleme
- Taille / urgence du marche
- Concurrents
- Pricing probable
- Differenciation
- Go / no-go argumente

## Etape 2 - Build 1 semaine

- Landing
- Offre
- MVP / ops minimum
- Paiement
- CGV / cadre legal
- Contenu de lancement

## Etape 3 - Launch 1 jour

- Site live
- Paiement live
- 1 post annonce
- 1 sequence outreach ou acquisition
- 1 dashboard de suivi

## Etape 4 - Operate

- Contenu
- Prospection / acquisition
- Support
- Facturation
- Amelioration continue
