# GEO Agency - US Market Scan 2026

Updated: 2026-03-13

## Short answer

Yes, the US market is already validating this category.

The strongest visible players split into two groups:

- enterprise AI visibility platforms
- hybrid agencies that sell AEO / GEO / AI search optimization services

## Core actors worth watching

### 1. Profound

- Type: AI visibility platform
- Why it matters:
  - strong enterprise positioning
  - strong product story around monitoring, workflows, and agents
  - strong market proof
- Success signals:
  - official site shows brands such as Ramp, Plaid, US Bank, and MongoDB using the product
  - official case studies claim material visibility lifts, including an 800 percent lift for Hone
  - SiliconANGLE reported on 2026-02-24 that Profound raised 96M USD at a 1B USD valuation
- Design takeaways:
  - dark product-led homepage
  - lots of proof blocks and dashboards
  - strong enterprise trust and event ecosystem
- Sources:
  - https://www.tryprofound.com/
  - https://www.tryprofound.com/customers/hone
  - https://www.tryprofound.com/blog/introducing-actions/
  - https://siliconangle.com/2026/02/24/profound-raises-96m-1b-valuation-ai-discovery-monitoring-platform/

### 2. Scrunch AI

- Type: AI visibility platform with agency partner motion
- Why it matters:
  - clearer mid-market and agency angle than Profound
  - more approachable product narrative
  - useful model if we want to sell both services and software later
- Success signals:
  - official launch note says they signed 25 customers in the first three months, including Lenovo, BairesDev, Crunchbase, Fictiv, and Penn State
  - official pricing page says trusted by 500 plus companies
  - PRNewswire announced a 15M USD Series A on 2025-07-22
- Design takeaways:
  - lighter SaaS design
  - strong scorecard framing
  - product screenshots drive credibility
- Sources:
  - https://scrunchai.com/
  - https://scrunchai.com/news/2025-03-scrunch-ai-launches-out-of-beta-to-help-more-businesses-win-in-the-ai-search-era/
  - https://scrunch.com/news/2025-07-announcing-our-series-a
  - https://ai-cdn.scrunchai.com/pricing/

### 3. NoGood

- Type: growth agency with AEO service line and Goodie AI tooling
- Why it matters:
  - closest model to a productized service business
  - very strong commercial site structure
  - excellent use of logos, testimonials, and case-study proof
- Success signals:
  - official homepage features recognizable clients such as Nike, TikTok, Intuit, MongoDB, Chime, and P&G
  - official AEO services page is mature and explicit about deliverables
  - official SteelSeries case study shows a concrete AI search positioning story
- Design takeaways:
  - bold dark growth-agency aesthetic
  - social proof very high on page
  - CTA and commercial momentum everywhere
- Sources:
  - https://nogood.io/
  - https://nogood.io/answer-engine-optimization-service/
  - https://nogood.io/case-study/steelseries-aeo/
  - https://nogood.io/aeo-course/

### 4. First Page Sage

- Type: service-heavy SEO / GEO-adjacent agency
- Why it matters:
  - not a pure GEO startup, but a very mature service business with strong client operations
  - useful benchmark for trust, support, and service packaging
- Success signals:
  - official site lists major clients such as Salesforce, Logitech, Verizon, and US Bank
  - official site describes itself as the largest SEO firm in the U.S. (self-reported)
  - official support portal shows a real post-sale service layer
- Design takeaways:
  - lighter, calmer, more executive style
  - content and process do more of the selling than flashy motion
  - support path is explicit
- Sources:
  - https://firstpagesage.com/
  - https://firstpagesage.com/our-process/
  - https://firstpagesage.com/support/
  - https://firstpagesage.com/seo-expert-san-francisco/

## What this means for us

The winning pattern is not "another SEO agency site."

It is:

- strong trust language
- strong proof language
- productized deliverables
- clear CTA path
- explicit support layer
- visible system, not vague consulting

## Recommendation

For our launch design and offer:

- take visual ambition from Profound and Scrunch
- take conversion proof density from NoGood
- take trust and service maturity from First Page Sage

Do not clone any of them 1:1.
Take their conversion patterns, not their brand assets.
