## SECTION 2 — WHY GEO NOW

### Title
**The way people search just changed. Your site hasn't caught up.**

### Body

Ten years ago, SEO meant: pick a keyword, write content, get ranked.

Today, a growing percentage of searches end inside an AI answer. The user reads the summary, maybe clicks one source, and moves on. That source is almost never chosen by keyword density — it's chosen because the AI system understood the site well enough to trust it.

This is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO). It's not a rebrand of SEO. It's a different problem:

- **Indexability** — can AI crawlers parse your content structure?
- **Entity clarity** — does the AI know who you are, what you do, and who you serve?
- **Citability** — is your content structured in a way that makes it easy to quote?
- **Trust signals** — does your site signal authority through schema, authorship, and references?

Most sites fail all four. Not because the content is bad — because it was built for human readers and keyword crawlers, not for language models.

We fix that. Systematically.

### Callout box
> **The shift is already happening.**  
> In 2023, ~2% of searches returned an AI-generated answer. In 2025, that number is north of 30% for commercial queries in English-speaking markets. French-language markets are 12–18 months behind. That's your window.

## SECTION 5 — WHY TRUST US

### Title
**We don't guess. We document every step.**

### Process

Every engagement follows the same sequence:

1. **Intake** — You submit your site, competitors, and 3 target queries via our intake form. No call required.
2. **Crawl & audit** — We run a structured crawl using a combination of automated tools and manual review.
3. **Human QA** — Every report is reviewed by a human before delivery. No raw AI output ships to clients.
4. **Delivery** — PDF report + action plan delivered to your inbox on the committed date.
5. **Review window** — 14 days to ask questions, request clarifications, or flag gaps.

### What "human review" actually means

We use AI tools to accelerate the analysis phase. We use humans to validate every recommendation before it reaches you. If we're not confident in a finding, we flag it as a hypothesis, not a certainty. You'll see the difference in the report itself.

### What we don't promise

- We do not guarantee that any AI system will cite your site. No one can.
- We do not guarantee ranking improvements. GEO ≠ SEO.
- We do not promise outcomes we can't measure.

What we do promise: a structured, documented analysis of where your site stands today versus what AI systems need to parse and trust it — and a clear path to close the gap.

## SECTION 7 — FAQ

**Q1 — What exactly is GEO?**

Generative Engine Optimization is the practice of structuring a website so that AI systems — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude — can parse, understand, and cite it in their responses. It covers technical structure (schema, crawlability), content format (answer-ready blocks, entity definitions), and trust signals (authorship, references). It's adjacent to technical SEO but targets a different layer of the web stack.

---

**Q2 — My site already ranks well on Google. Do I still need GEO?**

Possibly. Google rankings and AI citation are increasingly decoupled. A site can rank #1 for a keyword and still be invisible to AI systems — because AI systems don't use the same ranking signals. If you want to maintain visibility as AI search grows, GEO matters regardless of your current Google performance.

---

**Q3 — Will you guarantee that AI systems will start citing my site?**

No. No one can make that guarantee — AI systems choose what they cite based on opaque, continuously updated models. What we do guarantee: we identify the structural gaps between where your site is today and what AI systems need to parse and trust it. Closing those gaps is the best available lever. We document it, you decide how far to go.

---

**Q4 — How long before I see results?**

Schema and structural changes can be indexed within days. Changes to how AI systems cite your content typically show up within 4–8 weeks, depending on how often AI crawlers re-index your domain. We track a visibility score across key queries at baseline and post-implementation so you have a reference point.

---

**Q5 — What do you need from me to get started?**

For the audit: your site URL, up to 3 competitor URLs, and 5–10 target queries (the questions you want AI systems to answer using your content as a source). Everything goes through an intake form — no call required before kickoff.
