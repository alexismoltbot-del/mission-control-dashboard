# GEO Agency - Funnel v1

## Goal

Turn a visitor into a paying GEO client with minimal manual back-and-forth.

## Recommended path

1. Visitor lands on the site
2. Visitor understands the promise in under 10 seconds
3. Visitor clicks the dominant CTA
4. Visitor sees pricing and what is included
5. Visitor pays or requests the audit
6. Visitor creates access through magic link auth
7. Visitor completes intake
8. Team starts the run
9. Client receives updates and deliverables in the portal

## Primary CTA

`Launch my GEO audit`

## Secondary CTA

`See a sample deliverable`

## Friction to remove

- no vague service menu
- no hidden pricing
- no contact-only dead end
- no unclear next step after payment

## Required states

- pre-purchase
- post-purchase intake
- authenticated client area
- support/contact state
